Marangu Route

Duration: 6 Days

Price: $1700

Book on WhatsApp

Marangu Route Information

The Marangu Route is known as the "Coca-Cola Route" and is one of the easiest paths to climb Mount Kilimanjaro.

Travel Plan

Day 1 – Marangu Gate to Mandara Hut

Day 2 – Mandara Hut to Horombo Hut

Day 3 – Horombo Hut acclimatization day

Day 4 – Horombo Hut to Kibo Hut

Day 5 – Summit attempt to Uhuru Peak

Day 6 – Descend to Horombo Hut

Day 7 – Return to Marangu Gate
