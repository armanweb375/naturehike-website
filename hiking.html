8 Days Lemosho Route

The 8-day Lemosho Route is one of the best routes for climbing Mount Kilimanjaro. It offers beautiful scenery, excellent acclimatization, and a high summit success rate. The route begins on the western side of the mountain and passes through rainforest, moorland, and alpine desert before reaching Uhuru Peak (5,895m).
